Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Hebrew Verbs AI collects minimal information to provide our services:

  • App Usage Data: Learning progress, favorite verbs, quiz scores
  • AI Chat History: Stored locally on your device (last 5 messages)
  • Camera Access: Only when using the scanner feature, no images stored
  • Theme Preferences: Dark/light mode settings

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Track your learning progress
  • Provide personalized AI assistance
  • Save your preferences and favorites
  • Improve app functionality

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Room Database for verb data
  • DataStore for preferences
  • No cloud storage or external servers

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Pollinations AI: For AI chat responses (no personal data sent)
  • Google ML Kit: On-device object detection (no data leaves device)

5. Camera Permission

Camera access is only used for the scanner feature. No photos are saved or transmitted.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

Your data is protected through:

  • Local-only storage
  • No account creation required
  • No personal information collected

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Clear all app data through Android settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Disable camera permission anytime

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
